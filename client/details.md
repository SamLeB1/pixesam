### Tabs

#### Edit

Description: Includes operations that modify the selection or the active layer.

Features:

- Undo
- Redo
- Cut
- Copy
- Paste
- Clear
- Rotate (180, 90 CW, 90 CCW)
- Flip horizontal
- Flip vertical
- Transform
- Replace color
- Invert

#### Sprite

Description: Includes operations that modify the entire sprite, meaning all layers.

Features:

- Resize
- Crop
- Trim
